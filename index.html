Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Here's a timeline of his inspiring life:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from Saint Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.
  • 1960 - Joined DRDO as a scientist.
  • 1969 - Transferred to ISRO and became Project Director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1998 - Spearheaded the Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
  • 2002 - Elected as the 11th President of India.
  • 2015 - Passed away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong.
"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping it is something that does not let you sleep." -- Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
